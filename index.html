THE OPPORTUNITY

Where the Corridor Is Mispriced

Three structural opportunities remain systematically underpriced in the Asia–US corridor

01

Talent Arbitrage

World-class founders in Seoul, Tokyo, Singapore, and Bengaluru build at 1/3 the cost base of the Bay Area. Yet the moment they enter the US market, they're priced as outsiders

02

Distribution Asymmetry

US funds underwrite US revenue. Asian funds underwrite local revenue. Companies bridging both are systematically under-covered and over-penalized

03

Capital Cadence Mismatch

Corridor companies need investors who can endure geographic pivots. Most LP-backed funds can't. We can
